The Dawn of New Hope

On that first Easter morning, darkness gave way to light. The stone was rolled away, and with it, every chain of despair was shattered. What the world thought was an ending became the greatest beginning ever known.

Easter reminds us that no night lasts forever. No matter how deep the valley, how heavy the burden, or how long the wait — morning always comes. The risen Christ stands as eternal proof that love is stronger than death, hope outlasts despair, and life always has the final word.

Today, may that same resurrection power fill your heart. May you find renewed strength, unshakeable peace, and a joy that nothing in this world can take away.
